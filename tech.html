L'arcade (Popsicle Bridge Construction)

Welcome to the world of Popsicle Bridge Construction! This is not your ordinary building game - it's a challenge of engineering skills, creativity, and patience. The goal is to construct the strongest possible bridge using only Popsicle sticks and glue. You are on the mission to build your 'London bridge' that will never fall down but will bear the maximum load to be the winner.
